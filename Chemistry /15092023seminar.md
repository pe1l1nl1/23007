1. 1.25 moles of sodium hydroxide were dissolved in 450 grams of water. Calculate the mass fraction (%) of sodium hydroxide in the solution! (MNaOH = 40.0 g/mol)

**My solution**
```

m (water) = 450g

w (%) = m(NaOH) / total mass of the solution x100%
```

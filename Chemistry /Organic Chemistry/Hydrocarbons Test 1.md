## Aromatic compounds and Polycyclic aromatic hydrocarbons PAH
<img width="518" alt="Screenshot 2023-12-05 at 01 39 53" src="https://github.com/pe1l1nl1/23007/assets/19546253/ee857668-8755-4140-ba0a-1ca0022e6dc1">


**Groups:Phenyl (C6H5-) and Benzyl (C6H5CH2-)**

Most groups are either meta-directing and ring-deactivating or ortho, para-directing but ring-activating. 

PAHs = polycyclic aromatic hydrocarbons are built of fuzed benzene rings, including naphthalene, anthracene, and phenanthrene. 


<img width="527" alt="Screenshot 2023-12-05 at 01 42 51" src="https://github.com/pe1l1nl1/23007/assets/19546253/8b587b2d-e19d-4cab-b134-c4fff96b9d3e">

Toluene poses acute health risks, while PAHs are associated with long-term toxicity and carcinogenicity.
